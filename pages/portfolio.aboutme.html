About me

Hi, my name is Nanna Lybæk Bennedbæk. I am 31 years old and living outside of Copenhagen with my husband and our two sons. Fashion and design were my gateway to the creative industry, which led me to studying Multimedia Design. Out of my love for details and quality, my passion for functionality and technology evolved. After 10 years as a buyer in the fashion industry, the digital world won me over.
