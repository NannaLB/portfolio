Bridges in Copenhagen

About the project:

As a schoolproject, we were asked to take pictures of bridges in Copenhagen, and work on it in PSD, and place an object that wasn't there in the beginning.

Category:

Photography, Photoshop

The result:

I took various pictures of bridges, trying to capture the right one. When i decided which picture to use, i started the work in photoshop. The picture was quite dark, so i regulated it with lights, hue and saturation. Afterwards i removed the garbagecan, and inserted a ferry, making the water look as natural as possible around it.
